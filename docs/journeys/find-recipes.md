# Find Recipes

This journey guides you through the process of finding recipes based on your available ingredients.

1. Open the Zero Waste Cooking app.
2. On the landing page, locate the input field where you can enter the ingredients. Enter your ingredients separated by commas (e.g., tomato, pasta, basil).
3. Click the "Find Recipes" button. The button will be disabled while the app fetches the recipes.
4. Wait for the app to display a list of recipes. A loading message will appear during this process.
5. Browse through the recipe cards that appear. Each card displays the recipe title, ingredients list, and instructions.
6. Use these recipes as inspiration to create a delicious meal while reducing food waste.

Ensure your ingredients are spelled correctly for the best results.
